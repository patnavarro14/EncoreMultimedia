Classes in Dallas and Longview

LeTourneau's on-ground classes in Dallas and Longview and flexible online curriculum will prepare you for a rewarding career as a professional, faith-based counselor on marriage & family therapist.

A Degree of Difference

Marriage & Family Therapy is ranked as one of today's top careers, and LeTourneau's MAMFT, program provides the necessary academic requirements and clinical training you need to be LMFT or LPC license-eligible upon graduation. The profession of marriage and family therapy is protected to increase 29% over the next decade (Bureau of Labo Statistics).
